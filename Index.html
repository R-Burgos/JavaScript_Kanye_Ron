HOW IT WORKS

When you click the 'Auto Play' button, a text bubble will pop up with a quote from Kanye West and Ron Swanson. To simulate a conversation, each character will take a turn in 'talking'. Each text bubble will last roughly 10 seconds and have a 2 second interval between quotes.

Summary
This conversation simulator utilizes two APIs to simulate a conversation. The APIs provide quotes from Kanye West and Ron Swanson. Click the auto play button to generate a conversation. A redesign of a project from my time at TrueCoders. Initially created as a C# console application. (Click HERE to check that version out!)
